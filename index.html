Hi I'm Thasin. Welcome to Cus.Cur, a free custom cursor framework. You can get started by following a few easy steps given below.
  • First, click on the Copy common HTML button to copy the common HTML and paste it in the bottom section of your body tag.
  • Next click on the Copy JS CDN button to copy the common javascript CDN and paste it inside a script tag in your html head tag.
  • Last step, choose a cursor & click on its Copy CSS CDN button to copy the unique CDN and paste it inside a link tag in your html head tag.

& you're all set. Enjoy!
